FAQ

I really like the scans from VueScan. How do I purchase VueScan?

You can purchase VueScan online and get a VueScan serial number instantly.

I live outside the United States. Can I still buy it?

Yes, people in almost every country in the world have purchased VueScan online.

I don't have a credit card or debit card. Can I use a purchase order, check or cash?

Unfortunately, VueScan can only be purchased online with a credit card or debit card.

I'm having a problem with VueScan - what should I do?

The first thing to try is delete vuescan.ini and run it again. If this doesn't work, try to check whatever has changed between the time it used to work and now. There's a lot more info on the Support page.

I'd like to buy more than one copy of VueScan. How do I do this?

You can purchase multiple copies online.

Does my scanner work with VueScan on my computer?

The best way to find out is to download VueScan and try it with your scanner.

I've lost my serial number. How do I get another one?

You can get a new serial number online at no cost.

I have an older SCSI scanner on Windows XP. How can I get it working with VueScan?

Older SCSI scanners on Windows XP need ASPI installed.
